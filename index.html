Stopwatch

A simple stopwatch jQuery plug-in. Go ahead and put multiple stopwatches on a page. It works fine. :)

Example:

<div id="clock1"></div>
<div id="clock2"></div>

<script type="text/javascript" src="js/jquery.lumen.js">
    $('#clock1').stopwatch();
    $('#clock2').stopwatch();
  });
</script>
